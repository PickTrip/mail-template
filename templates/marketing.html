Discover the best winter city destinations

Cozy streets, snowy skylines, and unforgettable adventures — three city escapes worth bookmarking this season.

Japan

Sapporo

Step into a winter wonderland at the Sapporo Snow Festival, taste fresh seafood at Nijo Market, and ride the ropeway up Mount Moiwa for skyline views over the snow.

Czech Republic

Prague

Stroll the fairy-tale Christmas markets, watch snow settle over the Astronomical Clock, and warm up with mulled wine in the cobbled lanes of Old Town.

Switzerland

Zermatt

Ski under the Matterhorn, ride the panoramic Glacier Express across alpine valleys, and unwind with world-class fondue back in the village.
